The idea was before I even start to speak english, the master idea of everything in their own space as well as place. Multimedia database to be ready for parse &render any idea/content on a go to multiple devices in a real time. Also provide import/export all of playlists, render 2D map of connections between each other.

EMI - Elegant Mixing Interface

There was only one reason for this project, mess on my screen while I was mixing music in a DJ software program. So I just start from zero, find open source projects, forked them and edited for my needs and purpose on a go. Layers, over another, over another, etc based on position between points of mixing progress. Special custom mappings to fit sensitivity of my controller Behringer CMD-PL1.

Room23

From one point of view it's just combination of all my previous projects, from the other side it's just another level going more portable and green path. But more like it's just open source portable recording and DJ stage with party for like ~50 people on a go for like a whole weekend without electricity. In a small container, truck, SUV, Caravan... completely independent.
